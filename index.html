Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my design process.

What software do you use for your designs?
I primarily use the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop for raster editing and image manipulation, Illustrator for vector graphics and logos, and InDesign for print layouts. I also use Canva and Figma for quick social media assets and UI layouts.
Do you provide source files?
Yes! Depending on the contract, I provide all original, organized source files (PSD, AI, EPS) along with standard export formats like PNG, JPG, and PDF for print.
How long does a branding project take?
A comprehensive brand identity project (Logo, Color Palette, Typography, and Brand Guidelines) typically takes 1 to 2 weeks, depending on the revision cycles and client feedback speed.
