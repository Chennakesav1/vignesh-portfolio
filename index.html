About Me

My Background and Education.

I design visuals that speak.

Hello! I’m MRN Vignesh, a recent graduate in Graphic Design from JVS IT with a strong foundation in colour theory, typography and visual composition. During my course, I completed projects ranging from logo creation and branding studies to social media campaigns and poster design, which helped me develop a versatile design approach and an eye for detail.

As a fresher eager to learn and grow, I’m proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, and I’m always exploring new tools like Figma and Procreate. I thrive on collaborative environments, enjoy turning briefs into creative solutions, and am passionate about delivering on-brand visuals that speak to your audience. Let’s work together to create designs that leave a memorable mark!
